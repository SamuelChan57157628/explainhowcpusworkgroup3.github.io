Conclusion

To sum up, we have explored different stages involved in instruction processing as well as what CPU architectures are. CPU architecture is the design adopted in building CPUs and each architecture has its own advantages and disadvantages. After enriching the understanding and obtaining a deeper grasp of various architectures, it enables us to make the best choice in building up the computer system, which could ultimately result in optimizing the overall performance and improving the system efficiency.
